Passenger Rules & Regulations

1

Valid Ticket Required

Always carry a valid ticket or pass. Traveling without ticket is an offense.

2

No Smoking

Smoking is strictly prohibited in trains and railway premises.

3

Chain Pulling

Use alarm chain only in genuine emergencies. Misuse attracts penalty.

4

Luggage Limits

Adhere to prescribed luggage limits. Excess baggage requires payment.

5

Safety First

Don't lean out of windows, cross tracks, or board moving trains.

6

Prohibited Items

Carrying inflammable, explosive, or dangerous goods is strictly banned.
